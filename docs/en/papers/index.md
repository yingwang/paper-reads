## Four-section structure

Each note unfolds as:

- **The Problem**: what the paper sets out to solve, why it matters, where prior work falls short
- **Method**: the core design and key technical components, with symbols and equations explained in the order they appear
- **Experiments**: datasets, baselines, headline numbers, and the most informative ablations
- **Limitations**: what the authors acknowledge, and what a careful reader can spot beyond that

Each note closes with a one-sentence summary intended as a reading-list pointer for future recall.
